# CC and Armor Types

!!! warning "Patch-sensitive"
    Exact values may change. Verify current values with Albion Wiki, patch notes, or in-game tooltips before publishing hard numbers.


## What it means

Plate targets generally reduce CC value more than cloth targets. A tank spending a major CC on another tank may get low value compared with stopping cloth/leather DPS.

## Field behavior

Tanks and stoppers should think about enemy DPS paths, not only enemy tanks.
