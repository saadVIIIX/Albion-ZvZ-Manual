Many fights are decided by who recognizes the cooldown window after the first engage.

## Correct behavior

- Identify what the enemy is trying to make possible.
- Keep enough spacing to deny AoE escalation.
- Stay close enough to turn or support when the window opens.
- Use terrain intentionally instead of drifting.

## Common failure

Players react only to the last word they heard, not to the tactical situation. They move, cast, or defend without understanding the reason.

## Review question

At the key moment, did the player understand the fight state, or did they only follow a literal instruction?
