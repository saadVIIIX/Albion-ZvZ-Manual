# Site Review Checklist

Use this before sharing a preview publicly or with trusted reviewers.

## Navigation

- Can a new player find Start Here?
- Can a role player find their role page?
- Can an officer find workbook/tool pages?
- Can a maintainer find source/publishing rules?
- Do pages link back to related concepts?

## Content depth

- Are role pages practical enough to act on?
- Are fight concepts explained with behavior, not only labels?
- Are playstyle modules broad enough without pretending to be full comp guides?
- Are build cards clearly marked as workbook-backed and patch-sensitive?

## Safety / trust

- Is Khelron/AO University credited clearly but not excessively?
- Are private VOD examples anonymous?
- Are mechanics values linked or marked for verification?
- Are future package pages clearly placeholders?

## Preview check

- Open the preview zip.
- Open `index.html`.
- Click a nested page.
- Click a nav link from a nested page.
- Check text readability.
- Check that draft labels are visible.
