# Player Development Ladder

1. **Survive correctly** — do not clump, do not panic, use defensives.
2. **Stand correctly** — learn your role's normal space.
3. **Move correctly** — interpret direction calls.
4. **Press correctly** — use abilities on time.
5. **Pressure between engages** — Q/W/autos/support value.
6. **Read enemy intent** — know what is coming before it lands.
7. **Review yourself** — find one repeatable habit to improve.

A player does not need to master every step before joining content, but role leads should know which step each player is struggling with.
