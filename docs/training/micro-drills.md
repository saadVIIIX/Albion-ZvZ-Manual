# Micro-Drills

## Kite route discipline

Caller announces a route. Players pause the VOD and mark where their role should be five seconds later.

## Turn-window readiness

Pause after enemy engage lands. Identify who is safe and who should walk forward.

## Q/W pressure floor

During a brawl-control fight, track whether DPS are casting Q/W between E windows.

## Danger-area healing

Pause as enemy damage begins. Ask whether Holy/Nature/Fallen/Wild tools can reach the actual danger area.

## Support-layer timing

Track whether Locus, Oathkeepers, Rootbound, Demon/Judi/Guard, and personal defensives are layered on danger or wasted on safe space.

## Plate/frontline discipline

Track whether healthy plate is creating space or hiding in friendly DPS.
