# Public Beta Review Route

Use this page to review the public beta without getting lost.

The goal is to catch blockers, confusing wording, scope leaks, and credibility issues. It is not to expand the manual endlessly.

## Pass 1 - Purpose, trust, and framing

Read:

1. [Home](../index.md)
2. [Sources and Credit](../sources-credit/index.md)
3. [Known Limitations](../review/known-limitations.md)
4. [Workbooks and Tools](../workbooks/index.md)

Check:

- Is the manual's purpose clear before credits and source policy?
- Does the guide feel like it exists to help and teach the general Albion playerbase?
- Do Khelron / AO University credits support the guide without overwhelming it?
- Does the manual feel public/community-facing rather than guild-private?
- Is the workbook clearly optional tooling?
- Is the Great Fire Control Formation framed as an applied package, not the entire project?

## Pass 2 - New player path

Read:

1. [Start Here](../start-here/index.md)
2. [New to ZvZ](../start-here/new-to-zvz.md)
3. [Pick Your First Role](../start-here/pick-your-first-role.md)
4. [How to Read Calls](../start-here/how-to-read-calls.md)

Check:

- Can a newer player understand what to do first?
- Does the page avoid forcing them into one comp?
- Are the first habits practical enough to use in a real CTA?

## Pass 3 - Role behavior

Pick one or two:

- [Ranged DPS](../roles/ranged-dps.md)
- [Holy Healers](../roles/holy-healers.md)
- [Nature Healers](../roles/nature-healers.md)
- [Supports](../roles/supports.md)
- [Engage Tanks](../roles/engage-tanks.md)
- [Stopper Tanks](../roles/stopper-tanks.md)

Check:

- Does the page teach behavior, not only gear labels?
- Can a role lead use it for review?
- Are mistakes concrete enough to identify in VODs?

## Pass 4 - Fight concept clarity

Read:

1. [Movement and Positioning](../fight-concepts/movement-positioning.md)
2. [Counter-Engage Windows](../fight-concepts/counter-engage-windows.md)
3. [Defensive Layering](../fight-concepts/defensive-layering.md)
4. [DPS Timing](../fight-concepts/dps-timing.md)

Check:

- Do concepts connect to real player decisions?
- Are movement and positioning clearly separated where needed?
- Does the page explain positioning as the desired state and movement as the path/adjustment toward it?
- Are examples clear without becoming private VOD case studies?
- Does the guide preserve depth without overwhelming the reader path?

## Pass 5 - Weapon examples and patch-sensitive material

Read:

1. [Weapon and Ability Notes](../weapon-notes/index.md)
2. [How to Read Weapon Notes](../weapon-notes/how-to-read-weapon-notes.md)
3. [Patch-Sensitive Notes](../weapon-notes/patch-sensitive-notes.md)
4. [Mechanics Verification Queue](../audit/mechanics-verification-queue.md)

Check:

- Are weapon/item examples framed as teaching functions rather than forced current-meta recommendations?
- Are exact values, cooldowns, item behavior, and current strength claims treated carefully?
- Is source-check/historical labeling used where it prevents confusion, rather than repeated on every normal example?
- Is unsupported current-meta certainty avoided without making the guide timid or overly defensive?
