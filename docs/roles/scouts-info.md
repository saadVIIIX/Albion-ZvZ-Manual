# Scouts / HVT Watch / Information Roles


Information roles help the zerg make better decisions before danger becomes unavoidable.

## Purpose

Good information is specific, timely, and actionable. Bad information creates panic or false confidence.

## Useful reports

- enemy zerg location and movement direction
- enemy caller/front position
- bomb squad forming on a flank
- enemy rotating to a better angle
- high-value weapons exposed or channeling
- whether a group is mounted/dismounted
- whether enemy defensive layers have been used

## Bad reports

- "we killed a lot" without kill-feed support
- shouting every low target
- vague panic like "they are everywhere"
- reporting information too late to change the call

## HVT watch

High-value targets are not always low HP. They can be enemy callers, bomb weapons, exposed supports, clump tanks, or players enabling an engage.

## Practice drill

In review, take one scout/info call and ask whether it changed the caller's decision. If it did not, the report may have been too vague, too late, or not relevant.
