# DPS Overview

DPS is the role family responsible for turning fight windows into pressure, kills, and space.

The public manual should not treat one DPS weapon or one comp package as the default model.

## Core DPS jobs

All DPS players should learn:

- spacing
- range discipline
- timing
- target selection
- pressure between major cooldowns
- survival without abandoning the fight
- how support utility changes the kill window

## DPS families

Use these families to judge function, not only damage-meter value.

| DPS family | What to judge |
|---|---|
| Ranged DPS | angle discipline, E-window damage, Q/W pressure, safe uptime |
| Melee burst / clap DPS | timing, speed, entry angle, exit discipline, short-window damage |
| Melee pressure / DoT DPS | spread, sustained pressure, healer strain, forced movement |
| Support DPS / utility DPS | pierce, heal cut, purge, disruption, and enough pressure to justify the slot |
| Execute DPS | timing, target selection, and avoiding free deaths while finishing low players |

These are broad literacy labels, not fixed comp slots.

## Beginner DPS rule

For your first role, do not obsess over the perfect weapon category.

Learn:

1. where to stand
2. when damage should land
3. what target matters
4. how to keep pressure without clumping
5. when to stop running and help the counter
