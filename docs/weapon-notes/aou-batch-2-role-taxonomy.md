# AOU Batch 2 Role Taxonomy

Batch 2 adds a clean role taxonomy that should guide the public manual.

## Core model

| Zerg part | Episode framing | Public role layer |
|---|---|---|
| Armor | tanks | clump, disengage, kite, scout |
| Spear | DPS | melee burst, melee DoT, ranged burst, support DPS |
| Heart | healers | burst, HoT/sustain, pocket |
| Shield | supports | damage mitigation, mobility support, enemy disruption |

## Design impact

This is exactly the kind of public-manual structure we needed.

It lets the guide avoid bad simplifications:

- "tank" does not mean one job
- "DPS" does not mean pure damage only
- "support" does not mean one generic utility role
- "healer" does not mean only Fallen/Hallowfall burst healing

## Manual rule

Teach the broad taxonomy first, then place weapon/build specifics underneath it.

## Workbook rule

The Albion ZvZ Formation Ledger can use this taxonomy, but the taxonomy itself is public-manual doctrine, not workbook-specific doctrine.
