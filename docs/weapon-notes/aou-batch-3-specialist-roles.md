## Why this matters

Earlier pages could describe roles in broad terms. This batch makes them reviewable.

A reviewer can now ask:

- Did the disengage tank read the enemy before the engage?
- Did the ranged DPS create pressure between E casts?
- Did the melee DoT player stay active long enough to strain healers?
- Did the role use the whole kit or only the obvious E button?
