# Great Fire Control Training Notes

Training for this comp should focus on habits, not only build compliance.

## Priority habits

- maintain spacing
- do not panic-walk away after enemy cooldowns are spent
- keep Q/W pressure active
- land support utility before damage
- heal the danger area
- keep plate players forward when healthy
- kite as a unit
- review one lesson at a time

## Review method

After content, choose one repeated mistake, one role group affected, and one drill for next session.
