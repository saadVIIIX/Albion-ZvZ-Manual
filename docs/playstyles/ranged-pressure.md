# Ranged Pressure Concepts

Ranged pressure means creating value from distance through uptime, small-clump hits, area control, and timely conversion.

## What to watch

- spacing
- range
- pressure uptime
- cast timing
- target selection
- defensive pathing after casting

## Avoid weapon bias

This page is not a Great Fire, Frost, Longbow, or Brimstone guide. Those belong in weapon notes or applied build cards.
