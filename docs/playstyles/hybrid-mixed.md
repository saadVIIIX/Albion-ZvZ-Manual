# Hybrid / Mixed-Style Zergs


Most real zergs are not pure styles. They mix control, brawl, ranged pressure, bomb squad, and objective play.

## What this style wants

It wants players to recognize which threat is active right now.

## Examples

- control main zerg with a brawl flank
- ranged pressure group with bomb squad threat
- melee core with defensive/kite fallback
- objective comp that plays differently inside doors than open field

## Common mistake

Labeling the whole fight as "kite" or "brawl" and ignoring what the enemy is actually doing in the moment.
