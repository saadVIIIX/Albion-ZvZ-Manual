## What this style wants

Combines elements such as control main zerg, brawl flank, bomb squad, or ranged pressure package.

## Role expectations

- Tanks create or deny the path that this style needs.
- Healers cover the danger area created by the style's movement.
- Supports layer defense or utility on the actual fight window.
- DPS must understand whether they are pressuring, bursting, converting, or executing.

## Common mistakes

- Copying weapons without understanding the style.
- Playing a kite comp like a melee flood, or a melee clap comp like ranged pressure.
- Treating the whole zerg as one pure style when many strong groups mix styles.
