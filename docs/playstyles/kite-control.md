# Kite / Control Concepts

Kite/control style uses movement, terrain, denial, and threat to make the enemy commit badly.

## What to watch

- whether the kite preserves a turn window
- whether defensive layers are on the danger area
- whether tanks/supports protect the route
- whether DPS remain close enough to punish

## Common failure

Kite becomes panic retreat. The group survives but loses all counter pressure.

## Not a full comp guide

This page explains the concept; it is not a complete control comp sheet.
