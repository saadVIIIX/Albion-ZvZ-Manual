# Ranged Zerg Literacy

Ranged zerg literacy helps players understand a ranged fight shape without turning this page into a full comp guide.

## Ranged zerg wants

A ranged zerg usually wants to:

- keep enemies at arm's length,
- use standoff distance,
- hold and rotate between chokes,
- make terrain valuable,
- punish clumps with large AoE,
- use Q/W pressure to clean enemy tanks from the backline,
- avoid letting melee/flood pressure get inside.

## Formation idea

The episode emphasizes spread and crescent-style formation logic: ranged zergs can attack from angles rather than stacking into one melee ball.

## Strengths

- one-shot potential on clumps,
- safer distance engagement,
- strong coordinated damage windows,
- useful backline cleaning through Q/W pressure,
- terrain/choke value.

## Weaknesses

- cloth-heavy backlines,
- interrupt-prone casts/channels,
- positioning and target selection demands,
- collapse risk if enemy melee gets inside.

## Not a comp sheet

This page teaches literacy only. Specific build packages belong in Workbooks and Applied Build Cards.
