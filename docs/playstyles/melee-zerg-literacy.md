# Melee Zerg Literacy

Melee zerg literacy helps players separate burst melee from true pressure/brawler melee.

## Burst melee

Burst melee tries to:

- close distance quickly,
- land synchronized damage,
- exploit instant or hard-to-interrupt damage,
- flood only when the engage creates kills or advantage,
- reset when the engage fails.

## Pressure / brawler melee

Pressure melee tries to:

- keep sustained Q/W/DoT pressure active,
- spread out enough to avoid easy clump-and-dump,
- strain enemy healers over time,
- punish burst comps that survive only by resetting between cooldowns.

## Important distinction

A group can look like it is brawling because everyone is inside each other, but if nobody is applying meaningful pressure between cooldowns, it is not true pressure brawl. It is burst trading in close range.

## Strengths of melee

- fast engage windows,
- instant or hard-to-interrupt damage,
- resilience-penetration/melee commit value,
- strong punish when enemy spacing fails.

## Weaknesses of melee

- high exposure,
- higher skill floor for survival,
- more dependence on commit timing,
- risk of clumping into counter-engage.
