# Melee DoT / Pressure Brawl Comps


Melee DoT / pressure brawl comps win through sustained pressure, healer strain, anti-heal, and living close enough to keep value.

## What this style wants

It wants to stay engaged long enough that enemy healing and defensive rotation cannot keep up.

## Strengths

- creates constant healer pressure
- can make enemies panic or spread badly
- rewards durable frontline and anti-heal

## Weaknesses

- can be kited if it cannot reach backline
- suffers if players do not stay coordinated
- needs strong healer/support foundation

## Common mistake

Confusing pressure brawl with blind flood. Sustained pressure still needs shape and support.
