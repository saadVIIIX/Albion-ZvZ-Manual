# Melee Clap / Burst Comps


Melee clap/burst comps win by cutting distance and landing high-impact damage during a short window.

## What this style wants

It wants a clump, a fast engage path, and enough control to keep enemies inside the burst.

## Strengths

- punishes bad spacing brutally
- can instantly swing a fight
- strong when enemy defensives are late or misplaced

## Weaknesses

- predictable engage paths can be stopped
- bad first engage can feed escalation
- vulnerable to kite/control if mobility is forced early

## Common mistake

Diving deep without support, pierce, heal cut, or exit plan.
