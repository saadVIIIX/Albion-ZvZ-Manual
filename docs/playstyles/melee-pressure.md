# Melee Pressure Concepts

Melee pressure can mean many things: execute threat, sustained pressure, disruption, frontline punishment, or follow-up after enemy cooldowns.

## What to watch

- commit timing
- escape path
- enemy counter-engage
- allied support range
- whether the melee player is adding value or just feeding escalation

This page stays broad unless specific AOU weapon notes or verified comp data support more detail.
