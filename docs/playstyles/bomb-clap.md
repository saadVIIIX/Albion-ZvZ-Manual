# Bomb / Clap Concepts

Bomb/clap style tries to kill or heavily punish enemies inside a short window.

## What to watch

- enemy mobility
- clump tanks
- stacked DPS
- defensive cooldown bait
- where damage will land
- whether they can reset after the bomb

## Common response

If you survive the bomb and enemy cooldowns are spent, look for a counter window.

## Not a full comp guide

This page explains the style concept only. It does not provide a full bomb comp.
