# Brawl Concepts

Brawl style tries to stay active near or inside enemy space and win through sustained pressure, anti-heal, healing strain, and repeated value.

## What to watch

- sustained damage
- anti-heal
- healer pressure
- tanks/supports denying exit
- whether your backline can stay safe

## Common response

If the enemy wants to brawl, ask whether you should deny access, kite, split them, or punish their overextension.

This page gives broad literacy only.
