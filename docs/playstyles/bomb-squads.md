# Bomb Squads


Bomb squads win through surprise, speed, and punishing careless clumps.

## What this style wants

It wants isolated targets, bad enemy spacing, hidden angles, or predictable routes.

## Strengths

- punishes lazy backlines
- can win value without full zerg commitment
- creates fear and forces defensive respect

## Weaknesses

- can be stopped by aware tanks/supports
- often relies on surprise
- fails if enemy spacing and scouts are disciplined

## How to fight it

Tanks and supports must watch off-screen name tags, flank turns, and obvious bomb paths. Defensive tools should answer the bomb before it becomes a wipe.
