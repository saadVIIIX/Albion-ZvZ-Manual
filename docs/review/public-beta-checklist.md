# Public Beta Checklist

Before a public beta, check this list.

## Required

- Khelron / AO University credit is clear but not excessive.
- Private player names and guild critique are anonymized.
- Mechanics pages avoid unverified hard numbers.
- Build cards are marked patch-sensitive.
- Future comp packages are clearly placeholders.
- Workbook links are either live or marked pending.
- Page status labels are visible.
- New-player path is easy to find.
- Sources/Credit page is present.
- Maintainer pages exist but do not distract normal readers.

## Strongly recommended

- one trusted healer review
- one trusted tank/support review
- one trusted DPS review
- one new-player readability review
- one mechanics/build sanity check

## Do not publish as stable until

- core role pages are reviewed
- build cards are checked against current workbook
- mechanics references are verified
- the site has at least one clean public update note
