# Beta Candidate Review Instructions

Use this page to review the Beta Candidate without getting lost.

## Reviewer lanes

Pick one lane.

| Lane | Pages to check | Main question |
|---|---|---|
| New player | Home, Start Here, Pick Your First Role | Can a new player understand where to begin? |
| DPS | DPS Overview, Ranged DPS, Melee Pressure, Support DPS | Does DPS feel general, not Great-Fire-only? |
| Healer | Holy Healers, Nature Healers, Healer Deep Dive | Are Holy and Nature both explained as valuable? |
| Tank/support | Supports, Stopper Tanks, Defensive Layering, Role and Tool Library | Are responsibilities clear and reviewable? |
| Officer/reviewer | Training, VOD Review, Practical Examples | Can this become drills and review notes? |
| Source/credit | Sources and Credit, Known Limitations, Source Recovery Log | Is the project honest about source scope? |
| Workbook | Workbooks and Tools, Public-Copy Strategy, Great Fire Control Formation overview | Is the workbook clearly optional and applied? |

## Feedback format

Use this format:

```text
Page:
Section:
Issue:
Why it matters:
Suggested fix:
Priority: Critical / High / Medium / Low
```

## What counts as a blocker

- private names or private VOD identifiers exposed
- workbook master implied as public
- Great Fire Control Formation presented as universal ZvZ doctrine
- broken navigation from public paths
- raw markup visible in preview
- unsupported meta/current-mechanics claims stated as stable
- credit unclear or misleading

## What is not a blocker

- missing item icons
- future visual polish
- non-critical typos
- pages marked patch-sensitive
- advanced pages that need later expansion
