## Package

Great Fire Control Formation pressure-control.

## Role identity

Locus is a critical defensive layer. It should answer real pressure, silence/CC danger, or enemy engage moments, not be used as a panic button on safe space.

## Core jobs

- watch enemy engage path
- protect the actual danger area
- respond to real CC/silence/damage pressure
- coordinate with other defensive layers
- avoid being too far from the group that needs saving

## Timing notes

Locus that lands late may look good but fail to save anyone. Locus that lands on the wrong area is often worse than holding it.

## Common failures

- panic Locus with no real pressure
- Locus on safe backline while choke/front dies
- being too far from enemy center mass
- overlapping poorly with other defensive layers
