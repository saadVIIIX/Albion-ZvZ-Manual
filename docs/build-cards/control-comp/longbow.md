## Package

Great Fire Control Formation pressure-control.

## Role identity

Longbow is conversion pressure. It helps turn clumps, low targets, and enemy movement mistakes into kills.

## Core jobs

- stay at effective range
- prepare casts before the window closes
- keep spacing after casting
- use Q/W and autos when safe
- do not walk into the target before casting

## Timing notes

Longbow damage that lands late often hits after enemy defensives, healing, or movement already solved the problem. Cast so damage lands on the window.

## Common failures

- starting cast at zero
- standing too far back to hit the counter
- tunneling only on the caller's direction instead of the real clump
- ignoring safe Q/W pressure
- clumping with other ranged DPS
