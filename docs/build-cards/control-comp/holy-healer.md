## Package

Great Fire Control Formation pressure-control.

## Role identity

Holy healer provides direct stabilization and burst recovery. In this project, Holy is treated as part of a healer package with Nature, not as the only healer type that matters.

## Core jobs

- heal the actual danger area
- keep tank/front/choke groups alive
- stay close enough to affect the route
- coordinate with Nature zones and defensive supports

## Timing notes

Holy healing must reach the people making the call possible. If tanks and supports are holding a danger area, healing only safe backline players is not enough.

## Common failures

- standing alive but out of range
- abandoning the back through chokes
- trying to cover two danger areas poorly instead of one real danger area well
- not moving early with the planned route
