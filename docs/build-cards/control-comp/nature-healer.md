## Package

Great Fire Control Formation pressure-control.

## Role identity

Nature healer provides sustained healing value, zone coverage, and recovery through extended pressure. Nature is not useless filler; it fills needs Holy alone does not fully replace.

## Core jobs

- support the route and danger area
- place zones where players should actually stand
- coordinate with Holy and other Nature healers
- help stabilize extended brawl/control situations

## Timing notes

Nature value often depends on planning where the fight is moving, not only reacting to current health bars.

## Common failures

- placing zones behind the fight
- overlapping wastefully with other Nature zones
- treating Nature as optional
- being too far from the actual pressure area
