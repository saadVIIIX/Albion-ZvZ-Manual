## Package

Great Fire Control Formation pressure-control.

## Role identity

Great Fire is a pressure backbone. Its job is not only to land one big E on the caller's perfect clump. It contributes through frequent value pressure, Q uptime, Firewall utility, and repeated punishment of enemy frontlines or clumps.

## Core jobs

- maintain Q pressure when safe
- use Firewall to protect, split, or deny paths
- use E often, but aim for value
- pressure low targets and small clumps between main engages
- stay close enough to hit the next window without clumping

## Timing notes

Great Fire can often use E more opportunistically than Great Frost. If a real clump is forming and E is ready or nearly ready, prepare to hit it instead of throwing damage into one irrelevant target.

## Common failures

- panic-walking away with full HP after enemy cooldowns are spent
- holding E forever for a perfect clump
- using Firewall where it affects no path
- standing too far back to pressure
- stacking on friendly DPS
