## Package

Great Fire Control Formation pressure-control.

## Role identity

Oathkeepers provide defensive value to players in real danger. They are not a decorative backline support; they must be close enough to affect the area enemy damage is hitting.

## Core jobs

- stay near the threatened group
- protect frontliners, tanks, and players in the danger area
- use repeated resistance value when safe
- follow enemy pressure, not only caller's literal direction

## Timing notes

Oathkeeper value depends heavily on being in range. A perfectly timed support button does nothing if the Oathkeeper is on the wrong side of the fight.

## Common failures

- standing with safe backline
- leaving tank/frontline too early
- following the wrong direction call literally
- ignoring short-cooldown value between big moments
