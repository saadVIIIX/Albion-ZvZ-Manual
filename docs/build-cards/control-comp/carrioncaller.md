## Package

Great Fire Control Formation pressure-control.

## Role identity

Carrioncaller gives anti-heal pressure that helps sustained damage become lethal. Without heal cut, a pressure comp may create damage that enemy healers can recover through.

## Core jobs

- apply heal cut to the actual damage target
- align anti-heal with ranged pressure
- avoid wasting E away from the kill window
- keep pressure active between major engages

## Timing notes

Anti-heal late is usually low value. It should help create the kill window, not arrive after the enemy has already recovered.

## Common failures

- casting on the wrong group
- using heal cut after allied damage already landed
- chasing personal damage instead of enabling kills
- standing too deep without an escape plan
