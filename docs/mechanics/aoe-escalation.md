AoE escalation is why clumping is punished so brutally in ZvZ. If many players are hit by the same AoE, the enemy gets more value from the spell and the group becomes easier to delete.

## Field behavior

- Do not stand on friendly players unless a specific defensive call requires it.
- Avoid corners, walls, doors, and choke exits where your group naturally compresses.
- When an enemy engage misses or only clips part of the group, safe players should prepare the counter instead of joining the panic movement.

## Practical rule

If you are standing on three or more allies and enemy damage can reach that spot, assume you are creating a bomb target.
