Some mechanics here are highly patch-sensitive. The website should teach behavior, then link external references for exact current values.

## Healing behavior

- Holy and Nature should be treated as a package.
- Holy healing provides direct saves and burst stabilization.
- Nature healing/HoT coverage supports movement, sustained pressure, and people who need to keep fighting through chip damage.
- Healing the wrong area is still failure, even if the healer survives.

## Overcharge and disarray

These affect risk, effective power, and large-zerg scaling. Exact values should be verified before public release.
