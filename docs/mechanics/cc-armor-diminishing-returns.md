CC value depends on target, timing, and what the CC enables. A tank using major CC on another tank may get less value than stopping cloth/leather DPS or an enemy channel path.

## Field behavior

- Stop enemy DPS paths, beams, channels, clump paths, and bomb squads.
- Do not waste major stops on low-value targets when enemy damage is about to enter.
- If you are a stopper, stand between enemy DPS path and friendly DPS path, not just vaguely between zergs.
