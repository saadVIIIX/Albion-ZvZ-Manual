Focus fire / resilience protection affects how much value the zerg gets from stacking damage on one target. It helps explain why blindly chasing a single tank with the whole zerg can be low value.

## Field behavior

- Pressure front targets with Q/W, but do not waste the whole engage on an isolated protected tank unless there is a reason.
- Look for clumps, low targets, exposed healers, enemy DPS channels, and valuable support cooldowns.
- Scoreboard damage is not the same as fight impact.
