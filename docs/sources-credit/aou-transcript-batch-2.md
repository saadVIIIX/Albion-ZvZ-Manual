## Why this batch matters

This batch is a direct correction to the earlier problem of treating DPS/tanks/supports too broadly or too comp-specifically.

The strongest recovered framework is:

- tanks are not one frontline block
- DPS is not one role
- healers split by healing strategy
- supports split by what they protect, enable, or disrupt
- gear should be evaluated by the job it supports, not only by weapon name
